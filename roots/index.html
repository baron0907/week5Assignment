About Us

The CKHS rugby team is a high school rugby team located in Taiwan. They are known for their passion, courage, and teamwork with their black jerseys as their uniform. The players of the CKHS Black Shirts Rugby Team are respected for their great skills and physicality in the game and are known for their unrelenting spirit and solid tackling, their teamwork and tactical strategies make them a strong team in Taiwan Rugby.
